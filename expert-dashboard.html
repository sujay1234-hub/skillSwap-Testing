Tips & Resources

Advice to help you teach better, earn more, and build your reputation.

Teaching Excellence
Structure Your Sessions
Start each session with a 2-minute recap, cover your core topic with practical examples, and end with homework or next steps. Structured sessions get 40% better reviews.
Respect Time Boundaries
Start and end on time. If a student needs more time, offer an extension as a separate paid booking. This shows professionalism and increases repeat bookings.
Share Resources After Sessions
Send a follow-up message with links, notes, or exercises after every session. Students who receive post-session resources leave 5-star reviews 3x more often.
Ask for Reviews Politely
At the end of a session, simply say "if you found this helpful, a review would really help my profile." Most students are happy to — they just need the nudge.
Grow Your Earnings
Price Yourself Fairly
New experts do best at ₹300–500/hr to build reviews. Once you have 10+ reviews above 4.5★, raise your rate by ₹100–200. The platform average is ₹450/hr.
Keep Availability Updated
Experts with up-to-date availability get 60% more bookings. Update your available days in My Profile every week, especially before holidays.
Optimise Your Profile Title
Use specific skill keywords students search for. "Advanced Python for Data Science" performs 5x better than just "Python". Be specific about your specialty.
Offer Package Sessions
Suggest 4-session or 8-session packages at a 10% discount. Package students are 3x more likely to complete their learning goal and refer friends.
