The Problem

Education is
quietly broken.

Millions of talented students have the skills to teach — but no platform to share them. Meanwhile others can't afford quality learning. Both sides lose.

01

Education Costs a Fortune

Premium courses run ₹10,000–₹1,00,000+. Quality tutoring costs lakhs. Good education has become a privilege, locked behind a paywall most students can't cross.

02

Skills Die Unused

Students master Python, music, design, finance — but have no platform to teach, share, or earn from their hard-won expertise. Knowledge that could change lives sits idle.

03

Zero Trust Ecosystem

Finding a reliable peer tutor is a minefield. Scams, no-shows, unqualified teachers — without accountability, peer learning never scales past word-of-mouth.

04

Geography Limits Potential

The best teachers cluster in metro cities. Students in tier-2 and tier-3 towns get the leftovers. Your ZIP code should never decide your destiny.

Sujay Banarjee
Founder & Developer
B.Tech CSE — Lovely Professional University

A developer obsessed with solving real student problems. Started SkillSwap from a hostel room after struggling to find affordable tutors — and realising the perfect teacher was sitting in the next classroom.

Why I built
SkillSwap

In my first semester, I needed help with DSA. Every coaching centre wanted ₹15,000 upfront. I couldn't afford it — but my senior Ashish could teach DSA, and I could teach her Figma. We swapped. It changed everything.

"What if every student had a Ashish? What if every student with a skill had someone to teach — and earn from — at zero friction?"

SkillSwap is the answer to that question. Shipped entirely solo, growing by word of mouth, because the idea just makes sense for a generation priced out of traditional education.
